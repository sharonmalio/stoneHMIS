Invoice
-------
1. Click on the **Finad module** on the sidebar
2. Click on the **Sales** button
3. Choose the **Invoice** button and click on it
4. Search for the client whose invoice you would want to generate

Finance
+++++++

Manage accounts
---------------

**Adding a new account**	

1. Click on the **Finad module** on the sidebar
2. Click on the **Finance** button
3. Choose the **manage account** button and click on it
4. Click on the dropdown list from the top left of the page
5. Choose Item **add account**
6. Fill in the required details in the form presented
7. Click on the  **create account** button at the bottom and submit the details

**MOdifying Details of an exixsting account**

1. Click on the **Finad module** on the sidebar
2. Click on the **Finance** button
3. Choose the **manage account** button and click on it
4. Search for the account name whose details you need to change
5. Double click on it
6. change the details as required
7. Click on the  **Edit account** button at the bottom and submit the details

**Suspending an account**

1. Click on the **Finad module** on the sidebar
2. Click on the **Finance** button
3. Choose the **manage account** button and click on it
4. Click on the dropdown list from the top left of the page
5. Choose Item **suspend account**
6. Search for the account name which you would like to suspend
7. Double click on it
8. The status changes from active to dormant. (It has been suspended)


**Displaying all the available accounts**

1. Click on the **Finad module** on the sidebar
2. Click on the **Finance** button
3. Choose the **manage account** button and click on it
4. Click on the dropdown list from the top left of the page
5. Choose Item **accounts list**
6. All the registered accounts are displayed here.
