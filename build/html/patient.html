Patient functionality¶

6.1 Getting started with patient functionality¶

This is where all the patients activities are recorded.

6.2 All Encounters¶

All the patients waiting to see the Doctors or visiting the clinic in one way or another are found here.

  1. Click on the patient module on the sidebar
  2. Choose the all encounters button and click on it
  3. all the patints against their enconters and status of each is displayed here

6.3 Outpatient encounters¶

Accessing Destinations for the General Outpatient¶

  1. Click on the patient module on the sidebar
  2. Choose the Outpatient encounters button and click on it
  3. Click on the General Outpatient button
  4. All the Destinations are displayed here
  5. Click on the one you would want to use for the patients

PAediatrics¶

  1. Click on the patient module on the sidebar
  2. Choose the Outpatient encounters button and click on it
  3. Click on the Paediatrics button
  4. All the Destinations for the Paediatrics are displayed here
  5. Click on the one you would want to use for the patients

Obsygyn¶

  1. Click on the patient module on the sidebar
  2. Choose the Outpatient encounters button and click on it
  3. Click on the Obsygyn button
  4. All the Destinations for the Obsygyn are displayed here
  5. Click on the one you would want to use for the patients

Comprehensive Care clinic¶

  1. Click on the patient module on the sidebar
  2. Choose the Outpatient encounters button and click on it
  3. Click on the CCC button
  4. Click on the dropdown button at the top left.
  5. Click on the waiting list to view the list of those that have had encounters but not been attended to
  6. Click on the Review list item to view all the patients that have ever been to this destination.

Medicine¶

  1. Click on the patient module on the sidebar
  2. Choose the Outpatient encounters button and click on it
  3. Click on the Medicine button
  4. Choose on the button that you would like to administer on the patient

Specialty encounters¶

  1. Click on the patient module on the sidebar
  2. Choose the Outpatient encounters button and click on it
  3. Click on the Specialty enconters button
  4. Choose either the Child or the Adult specialties depending on the age of the patients
  5. Choose either of the specialties depending on the problem they got
